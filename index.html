Sukari Presbyterian Junior Secondary School

Sukari Presbyerian Junior School is part of the Sukari Presbyterian group of schools.The School was started in the year 2023 following the adoption of the CBC Curriculum and inception of the Junior Secondary Schools in the Country.

Address

Kericho Road, Kahawa Sukari in Kiambu, Kenya.

Total Number of Enrolled Students

There are 650 students in Junior Secondary and a total of 2500 students enrolled at Sukari Presbyterian.In each grade there are 6 classes represented as streams e.g 8P, 8Q, 8R, 8S, 8T and 8W.

Educational Board

The school runs under the Kenya Ministry of Education and The County Education Board.
